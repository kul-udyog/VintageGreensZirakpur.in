About Vintage Greens

A closer look at the vision, architecture and design intent behind Zirakpur's newest high-rise address.

Project Overview

A lasting legacy, built on trust

Vintage Greens is a residential development by Vintage Buildtech, spread across ten high-rise towers on Airport Road, Zirakpur. The project is positioned around a simple idea: homes that balance privacy, comfort and community, supported by generous landscaped open spaces between the towers.

Layouts are planned with well-ventilated rooms and aligned balconies, so that natural light and airflow reach deep into each home. The result is a design that treats every square foot as usable living space rather than wasted corridor.

Design Philosophy

The art of serene tranquility

Open avenues, tree-lined pathways and quiet courtyards are woven through the layout so that daily life feels unhurried. Vintage Greens is designed as a place to slow down — from morning walks along shaded trails to evenings by the water features near the clubhouse.

Every common area, from the entrance plaza to the garden pavilions, is designed to feel considered rather than left over.
