Vintage Greens Zirakpur — Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Vintage Greens located?+
Vintage Greens is located on Airport Road, Zirakpur, Punjab, near the VIP Road / Patiala Road junction, close to Chandigarh, Mohali and Panchkula.
What configurations are available?+
3 BHK (2,350 sq.ft.), 3 BHK+1 (2,698 sq.ft.) and 4 BHK+1 (3,796 sq.ft.) luxury apartments across ten towers.
Who is the developer?+
Vintage Buildtech, led by Mr. Gurpreet Singh Batra and Mr. Satish Kumar Arora. Their earlier project includes Tulip Heights.
What is the RERA number?+
PBRERA-SAS79-PR1181 — verify anytime at rera.punjab.gov.in.

Price Available on Request — pricing varies by tower, floor and configuration. Contact us for the current price list.

Yes — the community includes a dedicated pet park, as shown in the official layout plan.
