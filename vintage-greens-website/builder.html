Our Story

Building more than homes

Vintage Buildtech is a real estate development company built around the idea that homes carry trust, emotion and purpose, not just bricks and floor plans. The company is led by two partners whose real estate experience together spans over two decades.

Mr. Gurpreet Singh Batra brings a background in real estate along with experience in the hospitality industry, with a client-centric approach to project detailing. Mr. Satish Kumar Arora is known for a steady focus on quality and transparency across every development decision.

Their earlier project, Tulip Heights, is cited as a milestone delivery in the region's real estate landscape — a residential development rather than just a standalone address.
