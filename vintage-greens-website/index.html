About Vintage Greens

Where architecture meets everyday comfort

Vintage Greens brings together thoughtful planning and modern design across ten high-rise towers on Zirakpur's Airport Road. Homes are laid out for privacy and natural ventilation, anchored by a curved, glass-fronted clubhouse that houses the pool, gym and banquet hall.

The development is planned by Vintage Buildtech, a name behind the earlier Tulip Heights project, with a design philosophy centred on comfort, dignity and community.
